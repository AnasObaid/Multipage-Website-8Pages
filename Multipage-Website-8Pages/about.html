About Anu Quarantino

Welcome to Anu Quarantino Productions! We are a film production company dedicated to bringing unique and compelling stories to the screen.

Founded by Anas Obaid, a passionate storyteller and filmmaker, our company has been creating award-winning films since 2011. Our team consists of talented professionals who share a common vision and passion for creating high-quality films that resonate with audiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to tell stories that matter and inspire, whether they are documentaries, feature films, or shorts. We believe that film has the power to educate, entertain, and challenge people's perspectives. That's why we approach each project with a commitment to excellence and a deep respect for the craft of filmmaking.

At Anu Quarantino Productions, we take a collaborative approach to filmmaking, working closely with writers, directors, actors, and crew members to bring each project to life. We pride ourselves on creating a supportive and inclusive work environment that values diversity and promotes creativity.

Whether you are a film lover, a fellow filmmaker, or a potential collaborator, we invite you to explore our website and learn more about our company and our work. Thank you for your interest in Anu Quarantino Productions!

Established

Anu Quarantino Productions was established in 2011 with a mission to produce innovative and compelling films that inspire and captivate audiences around the world. Since its inception, the company has built a reputation for excellence in every aspect of filmmaking, from writing and directing to cinematography and editing. The team at Anu Quarantino Productions is committed to pushing the boundaries of storytelling and creating unforgettable cinematic experiences for viewers of all ages.
