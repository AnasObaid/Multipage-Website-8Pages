FAQs

Q: Who is Anu Quarantino Productions?

A: Anu Quarantino Productions is a film production company founded by Anas Obaid, a renowned filmmaker with over 20 years of experience in the industry.

Q: What kind of films does Anu Quarantino Productions produce?

A: We produce a wide range of films, from documentaries and short films to feature-length productions. Our films cover various genres, including drama, comedy, action, and more.

Q: Can I submit a script or idea for a film to Anu Quarantino Productions?

A: We welcome submissions from writers and filmmakers. However, please note that we receive a large number of submissions, and we cannot guarantee that your script or idea will be produced.

Q: How can I get involved with Anu Quarantino Productions?

A: We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team. Please visit our "Contact Us" page and fill out the form to get in touch with us.

Q: Where can I watch Anu Quarantino Productions' films?

A: Our films are available for viewing on various platforms, including streaming services, movie theaters, and film festivals. Please check our website and social media pages for updates on our latest productions and where to watch them.

Q: Does Anu Quarantino Productions offer internships or job opportunities?

A: We occasionally offer internships and job opportunities. Please check our website and social media pages for updates on available positions and how to apply.

Q: How can I support Anu Quarantino Productions?

A: You can support us by following our social media pages, watching and sharing our films, and attending our events and screenings. You can also donate to our production fund to help us continue making great films.

Established

Anu Quarantino Productions was established in 2011 with a mission to produce innovative and compelling films that inspire and captivate audiences around the world. Since its inception, the company has built a reputation for excellence in every aspect of filmmaking, from writing and directing to cinematography and editing. The team at Anu Quarantino Productions is committed to pushing the boundaries of storytelling and creating unforgettable cinematic experiences for viewers of all ages.
